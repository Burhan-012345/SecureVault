About Secure Media Vault

Version 1.0.0

Secure Media Vault is a modern application designed to protect your private photos and videos with advanced security features.

All your media is stored locally on your device and encrypted for maximum privacy.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All your media and PIN are stored locally on your device.

Permissions

The app requires access to your device storage to save and retrieve media files.

Security

All media is encrypted and protected with your PIN. We don't have access to your files.

Help Center

How to use the app

1. Set up a 6-digit PIN during first launch

2. Add media using the + button

3. View media by clicking on thumbnails

4. Use the action buttons to download, delete or share

Forgot PIN?

Use the "Forgot PIN" option and answer your security question to reset your PIN.
